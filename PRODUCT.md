# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Operators of autonomous AI companies (Paperclip users): solo founders and small teams who run org charts of AI agents (CEO, CTO, engineers, marketers) 24/7. Context: monitoring and steering — often glancing at a screen late at night to confirm the company is healthy, spend is under control, and work still traces to the goal. Job to be done: see the whole company at once, intervene surgically (pause an agent, approve a task, adjust a budget) without leaving the map.

## Product Purpose

Paperclip Atlas is a mindmap/orgchart command surface for Paperclip (github.com/paperclipai/paperclip), the open-source control plane for autonomous AI companies. Everything Paperclip manages — mission, org structure, heartbeats, budgets, hierarchical tasks, approvals — is operated visually on one living radial map. Success: an operator can answer "is my company healthy, and why is every agent doing what it's doing?" in under five seconds, and act on it in-place.

## Brand Personality

Precise, awake, calm. "Pre-dawn flight deck": instrument glow against deep cobalt — luminous signals on a quiet dark surface. Confidence through legibility, not spectacle; the drama comes from real state (heartbeats, spend, task flow), never decoration.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboard: card grids, hero metrics, gradient text, glassmorphism.
- Miro/whiteboard chaos: free-floating sticky notes with no hierarchy or state.
- Static org-chart tools (Lucidchart/orgchart.js): boxes-and-lines with no liveness.
- Dark-mode-as-aesthetic: darkness here is operational (ambient-light control room), signals must stay high-contrast.

## Design Principles

1. **The map is the interface.** No detours to settings pages — hiring, pausing, budgeting, approving all happen on the node itself. (TRIZ: local quality, intermediary.)
2. **Every pixel is state.** Ring arcs = budget, pulse = heartbeat, particle flow = delegation. If it moves, it means something. (TRIZ: dynamics, color change.)
3. **Zoom is semantics, not magnification.** Nodes change representation with scale: dot → ring → dossier. (TRIZ: nested doll, parameter change.)
4. **Why-chains are sacred.** Any task can light up its "because →" path to the mission. Alignment is visible, not asserted.
5. **Earned familiarity.** Standard affordances (panels, command bar, kbd shortcuts) around a novel canvas; the novelty budget is spent on the map alone.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on all text (≥4.5:1 body, ≥3:1 large) against the dark surface. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` alternative: pulses/particles become static state indicators. Keyboard: nodes focusable/tabbable, panel operable without pointer. State never encoded by hue alone (icons + text labels accompany color).
